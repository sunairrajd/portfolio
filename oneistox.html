Inspiration

In the summer of 2019, with 3 other classmates we tried setup a company ‘Oneistox’ (read one:X) from scratch. Our focus was to create unique learning environment for students that acted as a bridge between students and mentors , An idea to host offline workshops hosted across major cities in India bringing students and mentors together

Over the course of 2 months. we designed and developed interfaces, payment portals, marketing, advertisement and execution over the span of two months

Challenges and Learnings
  1. In today’s world, social media beats everything in advertising. Its more accurate, quick to execute and ability to iterate and create user-group targeted advertisements makes it better than any other medium.
  2. The return on investment using social media advertisement is extremely high.
  3. Its comparatively hard and time consuming to finish procedures for tax filing, registering an entity, opening bank accounts, for a new company
  4. One can create immense trust in a product by partnering with established institutions and people.
  5. Growth(website visitors/workshop participants) coming only from advertisements is highly unsustainable. Same goes for rewards and prizes.
  6. Tried and tested formulas quickly get copied and implemented by many.
  7. Great visuals = Good sales
  8. Don’t give up until the end
  9. It takes multiple triggers and approaches to convince someone to buy a product.
  10. Reputation comes from good visual communication. Nobody thinks twice, if the visual looks good
  11. Its just not enough to create a valuable product, until you know how to sell them
  12. Pack stuff well for transport if trying to move them geographically.
  13. The most common questions in workshops is ‘what next’ rather than why ‘why’
